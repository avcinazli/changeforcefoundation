Support Program

Career Change Support

Teacher, accountant, salesperson, nurse, driver — our graduates came from everywhere. Changing careers isn't starting from zero: it's transferring what you already know into a new field. We help you do exactly that.

Start Your Change

Why the Salesforce Ecosystem for Career Changers?

The Salesforce ecosystem is unusually friendly to career changers. The platform is learned by configuration rather than programming, the official learning platform (Trailhead) is free, certifications are recognized globally, and demand for skilled people consistently outstrips supply. Just as importantly, the roles reward business understanding — the thing career changers bring by the truckload.

A former salesperson understands pipelines better than most juniors. A former accountant reads data critically. A former teacher can train users. Our job is to help you see — and sell — that connection.

How We Support Your Transition

Personal Roadmap

An honest assessment of your starting point and a realistic, step-by-step plan towards your target role.

Skill Translation

Mapping your existing experience to ecosystem roles — and rewriting your story for tech recruiters.

Training Access

Priority access to our Salesforce Admin and Business Analyst training tracks.

Mentor Matching

A mentor who changed careers themselves and knows the doubts you're carrying.

Job Search Support

CV, LinkedIn, applications and interview practice — all tuned to the Salesforce job market.

Balance & Sustainability

Plans that fit around your current job and family — because most career changers can't stop working to study.
