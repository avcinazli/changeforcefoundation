Experience Program

Internship Program

"Experience required" — the wall every newcomer hits. We break it: our interns work on real Salesforce projects for real non-profit organizations, supervised by experienced professionals.

Apply Now

Real Projects, Real Experience

Our internship model connects two needs. Non-profit organizations need Salesforce help they often can't afford; our graduates need real project experience to land their first job. Interns join our consulting projects — implementing the NonProfit Success Pack, configuring CRM processes, cleaning and migrating data, building reports — always in a team and always under the supervision of experienced volunteers.

By the end, you don't just say you know Salesforce. You can point at a live system you helped build, describe the decisions behind it and back it up with a reference.

What You Take Away

Portfolio Projects

Documented, real-world work you can present in interviews — the strongest possible answer to "do you have experience?"

Team Practice

Working in sprints, using collaboration tools, giving and receiving feedback — how actual delivery teams operate.

References

A reference from project supervisors and, where possible, from the partner organization you delivered for.

NPSP Expertise

Hands-on knowledge of the NonProfit Success Pack — a niche skill with dedicated demand in the ecosystem.

Client Communication

Requirement conversations, demos and trainings with real stakeholders, not classroom simulations.

Social Impact

Your work directly helps an NGO serve its community better — experience with meaning.
