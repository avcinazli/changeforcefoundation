Salesforce Admin Training

The Salesforce Administrator role is one of the most common entry points into the ecosystem — and you don't need a tech background to get there. Our hands-on program takes you from zero to certification readiness.

Apply Now

Why Salesforce Administration?

Salesforce is the world's leading customer relationship management (CRM) platform, used by hundreds of thousands of organizations — from global enterprises to small non-profits — to manage sales, customer service, marketing and more. Every one of those organizations needs administrators: people who configure the platform, manage users and security, automate processes and turn data into reports.

Because Salesforce is configured with clicks rather than code, the admin role is genuinely accessible to newcomers. What employers look for is practical platform knowledge, problem-solving ability and the Salesforce Certified Administrator credential — exactly what this program is built around.

What You Will Learn

The curriculum follows the official Salesforce Administrator certification outline and is taught through real scenarios.

CRM & Platform Fundamentals

What a CRM is, how the Salesforce platform is organized, navigating Setup, and understanding objects, records and apps.

Users, Security & Access

Creating and managing users, profiles, permission sets, roles and sharing rules — controlling who sees and does what.

Automation with Flow

Building no-code automation: approval processes, record-triggered flows and screen flows that save teams hours of manual work.

Reports & Dashboards

Turning raw data into insight with report types, filters, charts and interactive dashboards for decision makers.

Data Management

Importing, exporting, cleaning and protecting data — Data Loader, duplicate rules, validation rules and backups.

AppExchange & the Ecosystem

Extending Salesforce with marketplace apps, and understanding the wider ecosystem of clouds, partners and career paths.

What's Included

  • Live instructor-led sessions — taught online by professionals working in the ecosystem.
  • Your own training environment — a free Salesforce Developer org to practice everything you learn.
  • Trailhead learning paths — curated modules on Salesforce's own free learning platform.
  • Certification exam preparation — mock exams, question walkthroughs and study plans.
  • Community support — a cohort of peers and alumni who learn and grow together.
  • A path forward — graduates can join our mentorship and internship programs.
