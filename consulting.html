Services

Salesforce Consulting

The most expensive CRM mistake is building the wrong thing well. Before a single field is configured, we make sure the plan is right — your processes, your goals, your budget.

Request a Consultation

Advice That Fits Your Organization

Salesforce is a powerful platform with many clouds, editions and add-ons — which is exactly why organizations need independent guidance. Our consultants analyze how your organization actually works, identify where the platform creates real value, and design a roadmap that matches your capacity and budget. As a non-profit, we have no license quota to hit: if a smaller solution serves you better, that is what we will recommend.

What Our Consulting Covers

Discovery & Assessment

Workshops and interviews to map your current processes, systems, data and pain points — the honest starting picture.

Solution Design

Which clouds, which edition, what to configure and what to leave alone — a solution architecture matched to your needs.

Roadmap & Budget

A phased plan with realistic timelines and costs, so you know what happens when and what it takes.

Process Optimization

Before digitizing a process, we improve it — automating a broken process just makes mistakes faster.

Governance & Adoption

Ownership, data quality rules and change management, so the platform stays healthy after go-live.

Health Checks

Already on Salesforce? We audit existing orgs and deliver a prioritized improvement plan.
