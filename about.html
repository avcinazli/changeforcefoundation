The Force Behind Change

We believe talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. ChangeForce Foundation exists to close that gap in the Salesforce ecosystem.

Our Story

ChangeForce Foundation is a non-profit dedicated to providing training, mentorship, consulting and resources to empower individuals from diverse and minority backgrounds — including migrants, young people and women — to gain access to career opportunities within the Salesforce ecosystem.

Founded in 2023 by professionals and volunteers from within the Salesforce ecosystem, the foundation grew out of a simple observation: the ecosystem has a huge demand for skilled people, while at the same time many talented individuals never get a realistic chance to enter tech. We bring those two sides together.

Today our team of 11–50 employees and volunteers delivers training cohorts, mentorship cycles, internship placements and pro-bono consulting projects for non-profit organizations.

Our Mission

To promote diversity, inclusivity and equity within the tech industry and to ensure that everyone, regardless of their background, has equal access to opportunities. We do this through hands-on Salesforce training, one-on-one mentorship, real project experience and consulting support for non-profits.

Our Vision

A Salesforce ecosystem — and a tech industry — that genuinely reflects the diversity of society: where migrants, young people, women and career changers are not the exception in tech teams, but a natural part of them.
