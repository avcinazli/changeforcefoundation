About Us

ChangeForce Foundation is a non-profit consulting organization in the Salesforce ecosystem. We provide consulting, implementation and digitization services with a focus on CRM and the NonProfit Success Pack — and we reinvest our work in promoting diversity, inclusivity and equity within the tech industry.

Working with us doesn't just transform your organization; it helps open the door of the tech industry to people who wouldn't otherwise get the chance.
